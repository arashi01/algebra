Companion object DistributiveLattice

trait DistributiveLattice[A] extends Lattice[A]

A distributive lattice a lattice where join and meet distribute:

  • a ∨ (b ∧ c) = (a ∨ b) ∧ (a ∨ c)
  • a ∧ (b ∨ c) = (a ∧ b) ∨ (a ∧ c)
